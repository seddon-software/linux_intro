Fish Pie
========

fish
potatoes
frozen peas
sprouts (or another vegetable side dish)
1 tblsp cornflower
1/2 pint milk for white sauce
1 oz margerine


butter top edge of casserole to stop potatoes sticking
cook and mash potatoes
cook frozen peas for 5 minutes
cook fish for 10 mins in 1/2 pint milk in a frying pan
flake fish and add to open casserole
add peas
make up white sauce (thickish) from milk used to cook fish and add to casserole
top with mash

place pie in middle shelf of oven and cook for 20 mins at 170C
